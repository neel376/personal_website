Overview

Methodology

Sonic Remake was made using the Unity 3D engine. This game's goal just like all Sonic games is to reach the end of the stage in as little time as possible.

This game currently features 3 playable character, Sonic, Shadow, and tails, each having different stats and wielding their unique special move.

I started this game near the end of 2013 during my junior year of highschool and plan on continuing in the near future.
